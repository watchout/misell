# 08. Misell Media Starter Kit

## Positioning

Misell Media is a facility-internal signage media concept. It starts from guide/promotion screens and can grow into regional ads or sponsor slots.

## Allowed Claims

- 放映回数を記録できる
- QR反応を確認できる
- クーポン/注文などMisellレール内の反応を確認できる
- 掲載後に次回改善の判断材料を作れる

## Not Allowed

- 広告収益保証
- ROAS保証
- 売上保証
- 視認者数保証
- インプレッション保証
- baseline/holdoutなしの増分効果

## Starter Inventory Example

| Slot | Duration | Unit |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Light | 15s | 1 week / 1 month |
| Standard | 15s or 30s | 1 month |
| Premium | 30s or 3-screen wide | 1 month |

Prices for ad slots require separate owner approval.

## Reporting Labels

| Label | Use |
| --- | --- |
| measured | Misell playlog, QR, coupon, order rails |
| estimated | external/assumed data |
| incremental | only with baseline/holdout evidence |

## Advertiser Report Should Answer

- continue or stop
- change creative
- change time slot
- change QR/CTA
- change facility/slot

## Legal Gate

Regulated categories require review: medical, financial/investment, recruitment, beauty/health efficacy, alcohol/age-restricted products, facility competitors, and privacy-sensitive content.
