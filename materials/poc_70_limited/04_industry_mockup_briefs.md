# 04. Industry Mockup Briefs

Common rule: show 3 real 16:9 monitors. Do not make an infographic. Do not put final price, legal claim, or outcome proof in the image.

## ホテル・宿泊施設

- Background: lobby or front desk.
- Monitor position: behind reception or visible lobby wall.
- Left: 館内案内 / 朝食 / 大浴場 / チェックアウト.
- Center: 周辺おすすめ / キャンペーン.
- Right: QR for map, reservation, coupon, or LINE.
- Avoid: luxury claims, brand logos without permission.
- Prompt: realistic hotel lobby with three horizontal 16:9 digital signage screens, left shows facility guide, center shows campaign visual, right shows QR guidance, clean Japanese hospitality style.

## カラオケ・エンタメ

- Background: reception or hallway.
- Left: room guide / event schedule.
- Center: food/drink/collaboration campaign.
- Right: QR for menu/order/coupon.
- Avoid: alcohol age-sensitive content without review.
- Prompt: lively karaoke reception with three digital signage screens, colorful but clean, center screen food campaign, right screen QR action.

## ジム・温浴・サウナ

- Background: reception, lounge, or rest area.
- Left: facility rules / schedule.
- Center: food, massage, recovery, ticket campaign.
- Right: QR for reservation or coupon.
- Avoid: medical/health efficacy claims.
- Prompt: modern sauna and wellness lounge with three digital signage screens, calm warm lighting, guide campaign QR.

## 貸会議室・イベント会場

- Background: lobby, elevator hall, or conference reception.
- Left: room guide / event list.
- Center: streaming package or sponsor message.
- Right: QR for check-in, floor map, or inquiry.
- Avoid: specific sponsor logo unless approved.
- Prompt: business conference venue lobby with three 16:9 signage screens showing venue guide, event sponsor style campaign, QR info.

## 飲食・小売

- Background: counter, storefront, or queue line.
- Left: menu/category guide.
- Center: featured item or limited offer.
- Right: QR for mobile order/coupon.
- Avoid: guaranteed sales/traffic claims.
- Prompt: casual retail food counter with three digital signage screens, product campaign in center, QR call to action on right.

## クリニック・待合

- Background: reception/waiting room.
- Left: appointment/process guide.
- Center: service information.
- Right: QR for reservation or patient info.
- Avoid: medical efficacy, before/after, treatment guarantee, misleading claims.
- Prompt: clean clinic waiting room with three digital signage screens, calm medical information design, reservation QR on right, no treatment claims.
