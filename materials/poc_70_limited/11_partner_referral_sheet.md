# 11. Partner Referral Sheet

## Who To Introduce

- Hotels and lodging facilities
- Sauna/spa/gym facilities
- Karaoke/entertainment venues
- Meeting/event venues
- Food/retail locations
- Clinics/waiting rooms

## Referral Pitch

```text
施設内の見られる場所を、案内・販促・QR導線を同時に出せる3連訴求面にするサービスです。
```

## Offer

- Misell 30日メディア化パートナー制度
- POC Partner: 初期150,000円 / 月額19,800円
- Normal: 初期398,000円 / 月額39,800円
- Tax excluded

## Partner Conditions

Referral/partner fee terms are not fixed in this sheet. Use `docs/77_POC_PARTNER_PRICING_AND_BUYOUT_STRATEGY.md` for internal planning and get owner approval before quoting.

## Good Referral Signals

- Existing monitors/posters are underused
- QR/LINE/reservation route exists
- Facility can allow photos/case study
- Staff can update content
- Installation burden is low

## Claim Boundary

Do not promise sales, traffic, ad revenue, QR scans, ROAS, impressions, viewer count, or incremental lift.
